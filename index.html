This is an image I created by recreating in Python the BASIC program consisting entirely of one line of code:
10 PRINT CHR$(205.5+RND(1)); : GOTO 10
then running it, and importing the results into Photoshop, setting it in Avenir Next and adjusting linespacing and kerning, and giving it Commodore 64-correct colors.
It's a program so concise and impressive there's a book about it.
